How to Play Set (Classic)

A Set consists of three cards where each feature is either the same on all three cards or different on all three cards.

Classic: Race locally with 2–4 players. Call "Set!", select three cards, then tap your player button to claim the point. If no one can find a set, tap No Sets to deal 3 more cards (up to 15 on the table). The game ends when the deck is empty and no sets remain.
